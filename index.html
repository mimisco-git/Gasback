Trust & Security

Built on radical transparency

Security and honesty aren't features. They're the foundation. Here's exactly what GasBack does and doesn't do.

Non-Custodial
GasBack never holds your assets. We never request signing permissions to move funds on your behalf. Connections are read-only for analysis.
Public Data Only
All wallet analysis uses publicly available onchain data. No private info scraping, no private keys, no non-public inference.
Opt-In Identity
Social and identity enrichment is entirely optional and user-authorized. Linked profiles are visible only to the wallet owner, never shared.
Transparent Eligibility
Every eligibility decision is explainable. See exactly which transactions qualified, which program funded your claim, and why others didn't qualify.
Protocol-Funded Claims
Claimable value comes from real partner programs, treasury allocations, and rebate campaigns. No unsustainable tokenomics or empty promises.
Audit-Ready
Contracts and eligibility logic are designed for independent audit. We publish documentation and methodology for all claim mechanics.
FAQ

Honest answers to direct questions

Is this a real gas refund from the blockchain?
+
No. GasBack does not refund fees already paid to validators or sequencers. Those fees are immutable. GasBack matches your gas history with active rebate programs and protocol reward campaigns, similar to how cashback or loyalty programs work but for onchain activity.
How is claimable value calculated?
+
The eligibility engine cross-references your gas history with active campaigns from protocol partners. Eligibility depends on which protocols you've used, activity level, chain, transaction types, and timing relative to campaign windows. Full methodology is documented in our Docs.
Which EVM chains are supported?
+
Currently: Ethereum Mainnet, Arbitrum One, Base, Optimism, and Polygon PoS. We continuously expand as new protocol programs launch. Chain coverage depends on active partnerships and available campaign funding per network.
Is my wallet safe when I connect?
+
Yes. Wallet connection is strictly read-only. GasBack only reads your public transaction history from indexed onchain data. We never request message signing, token approvals, or any permission that could allow fund movement. Your private keys never leave your wallet.
Do you access my private data?
+
No. Every data point used comes from public blockchain records. Wallet balances, transaction hashes, interacted protocols, and gas usage are all publicly visible onchain. We do not infer, collect, or store any private information about wallet owners.
Why is some of my gas spend not eligible?
+
Eligibility is determined by active campaigns. A transaction is eligible when it falls within a campaign window and involves a partnered protocol. Transactions outside campaign periods, on unsupported chains, or without active programs will not generate claimable value. This is always clearly explained in your dashboard.
How often can I claim?
+
Whenever eligible value is available in your claim queue. No mandatory wait period. As new campaigns activate and your activity qualifies, value accumulates. You can claim daily, weekly, or whenever your balance reaches a meaningful amount. Minimum claim thresholds may apply per program.
How are linked profiles displayed?
+
Identity enrichment is entirely opt-in. Link your ENS, Farcaster, Lens, or social profiles to your GasBack dashboard. These are shown only to you when logged in and are never shared with third parties, indexed publicly on our platform, or used for targeting.
