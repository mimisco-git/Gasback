FAQ

Honest answers to
direct questions

Is this a gas refund or refund of fees already paid?
+
No. GasBack does not refund fees already paid to validators or sequencers. Those transactions are immutable and those fees are gone. GasBack works by matching your gas history with active rebate programs, protocol reward campaigns, and treasury-funded support — similar to how cashback or loyalty programs work, but for onchain activity.
How is claimable value calculated?
+
Our eligibility engine cross-references your gas history with active campaigns from protocol partners. Eligibility depends on factors like which protocols you've interacted with, your activity level, the chain, transaction types, and timing relative to campaign windows. Full methodology is documented in our Docs.
Which EVM chains are supported?
+
Currently: Ethereum Mainnet, Arbitrum One, Base, Optimism, and Polygon PoS. We continuously expand as new protocol programs launch. Chain coverage depends on active partnerships and available campaign funding for each network.
Is my wallet safe when I connect?
+
Yes. Wallet connection is strictly read-only. GasBack only reads your public transaction history from indexed onchain data. We never request message signing, token approvals, or any permission that could allow fund movement. Your private keys never leave your wallet.
Do you access my private data?
+
No. Every data point used comes from public blockchain records. Wallet balances, transaction hashes, interacted protocols, and gas usage are all publicly visible on-chain. We do not infer, collect, or store any private information about wallet owners.
Why is some of my gas spend not eligible?
+
Eligibility is determined by active campaigns. A transaction is eligible when it falls within a campaign window and involves a partnered protocol. Transactions outside campaign periods, on unsupported chains, or with protocols without active programs will not generate claimable value — this is always clearly explained in your dashboard.
How often can I claim?
+
Whenever eligible value is available in your claim queue. There's no mandatory wait period. As new campaigns activate and your activity qualifies, value accumulates. You can claim daily, weekly, or whenever your balance reaches a meaningful amount. Minimum claim thresholds may apply per program.
How are linked profiles displayed?
+
Identity enrichment is entirely opt-in. You can choose to link your ENS, Farcaster, Lens, or social profiles to your GasBack dashboard. These are displayed only to you when logged in and are never shared with third parties, indexed publicly on our platform, or used for targeting.
